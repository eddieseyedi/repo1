Community News

Fall Neighborhood Cleanup Day

October 15, 2025

Join your neighbors for our annual fall cleanup! We’ll meet at the community entrance at 9:00 AM to pick up litter and tidy up common areas. Trash bags and gloves will be provided. Refreshments and snacks available afterward.

Upcoming HOA Meeting

November 12, 2025

Our next HOA board meeting will take place at 7:00 PM in the clubhouse. All residents are welcome to attend. We’ll discuss upcoming maintenance projects and review the annual budget.

Holiday Decoration Contest

December 1, 2025

Get ready to spread some holiday cheer! The annual decoration contest begins December 1st. Prizes will be awarded for the most festive and creative homes. Judging will take place the week of December 18th.
